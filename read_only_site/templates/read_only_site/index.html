Welcome to the Hard Drop League!

How do I register?

Go to the Hard Drop Discord ( https://discord.gg/harddrop), and type !register [jstris handle] in the #compete channel.

How do I submit my score?

The loser of the match has to type !winner @mention, with @mention mentionning the winner.

What kind of matches can I play?

It's up to you! As long as you play jstris (https://jstris.jezevec10.com/) and agree with the other player what kind of format you want to play, it's fine! The base format should be first to 7.

What is the ranking?

The ranking is a number determining your rank. The higher the better! You win ranking points by winning matches, and lose ranking points by losing matches. A highly ranked player will lose a lot of points if they lose against a lower ranked player; but a low ranked player will win a lot of points if they win against a highly ranked player! The more matches you do, the more your ranking will match your actual rank.
